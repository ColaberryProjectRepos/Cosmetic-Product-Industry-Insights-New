Problem Statement 2


The cosmetic product industry faces significant challenges due to fluctuations in company count, chemical count, and product discontinuation rates year over year, with some metrics showing decreases of up to 71% and others increasing by as much as 124%. These fluctuations impact business performance and market competitiveness, highlighting the need for strategic planning and adaptation. Understanding the underlying factors driving these fluctuations and developing targeted strategies to mitigate their negative effects while capitalizing on emerging opportunities is essential for sustained growth and success in the industry. Innovation, market responsiveness, and tailored approaches to product development and marketing are key factors in addressing these challenges and driving future growth.
