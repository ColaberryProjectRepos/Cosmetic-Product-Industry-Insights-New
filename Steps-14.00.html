KPI Cards and Sub KPIs 14

Let's create the KPI cards along with their sub KPIs. 
  1. First, copy and paste the Refresh Date Card. Then, adjust the size properties to a height of 135 and width of 212. 
  2. Change the title of the card to reflect the respective KPI. Use the colors specified in the Colors.txt file within the Project Essentials folder. Also, adjust the field attributes based on the data they represent. 
  3. For the sub KPIs, drag and drop another card with a size property of height 35 and width 60. Place them at the rightmost side of their respective KPI. 
Follow the GIF attached for further instructions. Additionally, ensure that the "discontinued_product_percentage" measure and all sub KPI measures are formatted as percentages and rounded up to 0 decimal places.
