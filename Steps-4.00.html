Key Insights 4

Positive Insights





  • Despite fluctuations in company count and product discontinuation rates, the cosmetic industry experienced a substantial increase in product count in 2019, with a growth of 80% compared to the previous year. This indicates a strong focus on innovation and product development within the industry. 


  • In 2013, despite a slight decrease in overall company count, the industry saw a notable 34% increase in company presence. This suggests promising market expansion opportunities and a growing demand for cosmetic products in these region


Negative Insights






  • Despite a promising increase in chemical count in 2013, the industry saw a sharp decline of 46% in 2010, indicating potential challenges in product formulation and regulatory compliance during that period.


  • The year 2016 witnessed a significant decrease in company count by 6%, highlighting potential market consolidation and challenges faced by smaller players in the industry.


  • Product discontinuation rates saw a notable increase of 156% in 2018 compared to the previous year, signalling potential issues with product relevance, market demand, or manufacturing constraints.


  • In 2020, there was a substantial decrease in discontinued product count by 80%, indicating potential disruptions in supply chain operations or challenges in product portfolio management amidst the global pandemic.
